## About

I run [Total Ventures](https://totalventures.io) — a product studio building AI products and the infrastructure to run them.

I built [Inky](https://inkyverse.io) because I wanted better bedtime stories for my son. It's now a real platform: iOS app, Gemini-powered story generation, word-level read-along narration, a growing story library. That's roughly how I approach most things — start with something I want to exist, build it, see if it holds up.

Previously a frontend engineer at [Fender](https://fender.com), shipping eCommerce for Fender, Jackson, and PreSonus, and building LEO — an internal Claude-powered CLI that ran our Jira workflow end-to-end. Thirteen years in the U.S. Army National Guard before that.

Open to engineering roles at small teams moving fast.

<br />

## What I'm Building

**[Inky](https://inkyverse.io)** — AI storytelling app for kids. iOS and web. Gemini + Imagen pipeline.

**[Pregnancy Power Hour](https://pregnancypowerhour.com)** — My wife's consulting business. Content pipeline turns one video into a blog post, newsletter, and social.

**[The F1 Formula](https://thef1formula.com)** — Automated F1 news site. RSS to AI summary to published, plus a newsletter.

**VERA** — My agent stack. Claude skills, specialist agents, and a CLI that runs the studio.
