## About

I run [Total Ventures](https://totalventures.io) — a digital product studio I built and operate solo, with AI as the team. Three live revenue streams, seven products, eleven shared TypeScript packages, one person.

I taught myself to code three years ago. Before that: 14 years as a working jazz guitarist (500+ events, ran my own bookings), 13 years in the Army National Guard (senior NCO, logistics), operations lead for a real estate team that closed $9M in a year, production work on Super Bowl LVI commercials. No CS degree. No bootcamp. I learned by building things that make money.

Most recently a Full-Stack Engineer at Fender, where I shipped multi-brand eCommerce across Fender, Jackson, and PreSonus and built **LEO** — an internal Claude-powered CLI that turned the team's Jira workflow into orchestrated agentic delivery. That prototype is the seed of everything I build now.

Open to Senior / Staff Engineer roles at Series A–C teams building AI-native products.

## What I'm Building

**[Inky](https://inkyverse.io)** — AI storytelling app for kids. Native iOS (Swift 6 / SwiftUI) + Next.js web + Firebase. Multi-stage Gemini + Imagen 4 generation pipeline with quality validation, credit reservation, safety filtering, and auto-retry. Word-level read-along narration. Dual monetization via RevenueCat (iOS) and Stripe (web), with a Pub/Sub budget kill switch.

**[Pregnancy Power Hour](https://pregnancypowerhour.com)** — Consulting platform with a Claude-powered content engine. One transcript becomes a week of content: SEO blog, newsletter, social posts, short-form clip strategies, with one-click publishing to YouTube and Instagram. Multi-step Resend sequences with conditional branching. Google Ads funnels with end-to-end conversion tracking.

**[Total Formula 1 World](https://thef1formula.com)** — Automated media property. RSS → Gemini summarization → Firestore → AdSense-monetized site + scheduled newsletter. The pilot for a network of niche media sites — a reusable site-template, link registry, and content engine all factored back into shared packages.

**VERA** — Production multi-agent engineering system that runs the studio. 27 custom skills, 4 specialist auditor agents, a structured session protocol (`vera context` → implement → `vera done`), and a single Go binary CLI (Cobra + BubbleTea) with six domain-scoped interfaces for deployments, analytics, ads, finance, and ops. Not a demo — it's the operating system this whole studio runs on.

## How I Work

- **Specifics beat abstractions.** "Migrated 14 callables from v2 HTTPS to onCall and shaved 300ms off cold start" beats "Optimized our backend."
- **Profit before revenue, cash before vanity, craft before scale.** Small, well-run, durable beats big, fast, brittle.
- **One good thing for a long time** beats ten okay things for a quarter.
- **Working in public.** Shipping today, learning the hard way, agentic engineering — that's the loop.
